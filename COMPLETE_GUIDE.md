## ğŸ“Œ Pinterest Marketing Strategy

### Pin Creation Formula:

**1. Headline (most important)**
- Use numbers: "7 Ways..." or "Top 10..."
- Include benefit: "...to Make Money Fast"
- Create curiosity: "You Won't Believe #5"

**2. Subheadline**
- Elaborate on benefit
- Use action words
- Keep it short (5-10 words)

**3. Visual**
- Eye-catching colors
- High contrast
- Large, readable text
- Lifestyle or aspirational images

**4. Branding**
- Your logo/website name
- Consistent color scheme
- Same font family

### Pin Specifications:

**Dimensions:**
- Optimal: 1000 x 1500 pixels (2:3 ratio)
- Also works: 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio)

**File Format:**
- PNG (best quality)
- JPG (smaller file size)

**File Size:**
- Under 20 MB
- Smaller is better for loading speed

### Pinning Schedule:

**Daily Tasks (15-30 minutes):**
- Post 5-10 new pins
- Repin 10-15 relevant pins
- Engage with 20-30 pins (like, comment)
- Follow 10-20 new accounts in your niche

**Weekly Tasks (2-3 hours):**
- Create 20-30 new pin designs
- Write 1-2 new blog posts
- Optimize top-performing pins
- Join 3-5 new group boards

**Monthly Tasks:**
- Analyze Pinterest Analytics
- Update underperforming content
- Research trending topics
- Plan next month's content

### Tools You Need:

**Design:**
- Canva (free) - Create pin designs
- Remove.bg (free) - Remove image backgrounds
- Coolors.co (free) - Color palette generator

**Scheduling:**
- Tailwind (free trial, then $15/month) - Best Pinterest scheduler
- Pinterest Native Scheduler (free) - Basic scheduling

**Keywords:**
- Pinterest Search Bar (free) - See trending searches

**Analytics:**
- Pinterest Analytics (free) - Built-in stats
